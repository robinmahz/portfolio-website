About Me

Hello! I'm Robin Maharjan, a passionate web developer and designer from Thecho, Nepal. I specialize in creating clean, efficient, and visually appealing websites. With a strong foundation in both front-end and back-end technologies, I aim to craft digital experiences that not only look great but also perform exceptionally well.

I have experience working with a variety of tools and frameworks, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Tailwind, Mysql and Laravel. Whether it's building responsive websites from scratch or optimizing existing projects, I take pride in delivering quality work that meets the needs of my clients.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring new design trends, learning the latest in web development, or enjoying the beautiful landscapes of Nepal.
